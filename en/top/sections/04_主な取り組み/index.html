Our Approach

Core Practices

At Kameya,

we create points of contact between people and traditional craft, carrying that value into the future.

01

Connect

Cultural Events

Creating meaningful encounters with traditional craft

Through lectures, exhibitions, studio visits, and hands-on experiences, we create encounters where people can touch the stories and techniques behind each work.

  • Lectures and exhibitions by Living National Treasures
  • Studio visits and experience planning
  • Guided viewing experience design

02

Integrate

Cultural Space Design

Bringing craft into spaces with cultural intent

We design spaces that incorporate the dignity and stories of Japanese craft into companies and facilities, leaving a lasting memory for visitors.

  • Planning for companies and facilities
  • Japanese craft introduction support

03

Create

Product Planning

Shaping products that carry the maker's intent

By combining traditional techniques with contemporary sensibility, we create valuable products that fit everyday life.

  • Sales and commissioned production
  • Original product development

These practices are not one-off projects. Each one is designed so people can understand, touch, and receive the value of craft in daily life.

We work directly with makers and keep listening to their voices as we plan. Connecting craft to the future means delivering it in a form that has meaning for the artists as well.

Kameya Japan Tradition Co., Ltd. brings Japanese craft from something only protected toward something continuously chosen.
