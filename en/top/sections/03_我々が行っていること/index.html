Our Work

What We Do

We create encounters where Japanese craft becomes part of everyday life,
and draws people in naturally,
through collaborations across industries.
We honor the background of each material and technique.

We work with artists including Living National Treasures,
who carry some of today's highest levels of craft,
as we seek forms where craft can live within daily time.

Example Collaborating Artists

  • Ryozo Kawakita, woodcraft, Living National Treasure
  • Ito Sekisui V, ceramics, Living National Treasure
  • Nobuo Matsubara, dyeing and weaving, Living National Treasure
  • and others
