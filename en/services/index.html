Service Areas

Services

Connecting craft
with contemporary life.

We design places where not only the works are seen, but also the techniques, stories, and attitudes of the makers behind them can be received.
We increase the opportunities for people and craft to meet, creating a flow in which culture is chosen within contemporary life and business.

Cultural Event Planning

Creating meaningful encounters with traditional craft

Through lectures and exhibitions by artists including Living National Treasures, we provide opportunities to encounter the essence of craft. We combine studio visits, hands-on experiences, and guided viewing to design places where the maker's skill and spirit can be felt.

Cultural Space Design

Bringing craft into spaces with cultural intent

We plan craft introductions for corporate offices, hotels, cultural facilities, and other spaces. Rather than treating craft as decoration, we support introductions that leave cultural stories and Japanese aesthetics in line with the purpose of the space and the visitor experience.

Product Planning & Sales

Shaping works that carry the maker's intent

Through sales of artists' and studios' works, commissioned production, and original product planning, we deliver the intentions of makers to those who use the works. By combining traditional techniques with contemporary sensibilities, we propose value that continues to be chosen in daily life.

Network

Projects & Artists

With makers
across Japan.

Through ongoing dialogue with makers who carry regional traditions, we create pathways for works to reach the places where they belong.
We reconnect making rooted in regional culture and technique to contemporary spaces, experiences, and products.
